Welcome! This is the event page for the July 2018 Berlin Council. Please follow the Berlin Council planning wiki for active discussions or join the Gitter room. Important news items and updates will be posted below. The About page has more background.

  • When: July 9 - 10 or July 14 - 15
  • Where: Berlin, Germany - Venue TBD; livestream
  • What: technical discussions & technical governance of the Ethereum project and related code
  • Who: Anyone with an interest in improving Ethereum technically, nurturing community consensus on technical direction, evolving high quality Ethereum Improvement Proposals (EIPs)

Please use _#berlincouncil2018_ across platforms to help share resources and find each other in online discussions.
